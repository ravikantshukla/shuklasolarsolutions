Frequently Asked Questions

A 1 kW system costs approximately ₹60,000–₹75,000 before subsidy. With the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana subsidy (up to ₹78,000 for 3+ kW systems), your effective cost is significantly lower. We provide detailed quotes after a free site survey.

Typically 2–3 days for residential systems. This includes mounting, wiring, inverter installation, and commissioning. Net metering approval from DISCOM may take an additional 2–4 weeks, which we handle for you.

With current electricity rates and government subsidies, most residential systems pay for themselves in 3–4 years. After that, you enjoy free electricity for the remaining 21+ years of the panel's 25-year warranty period.

Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days at reduced efficiency (15–25%). On-grid systems draw from the grid when needed and earn credits when you produce excess. Battery backup systems keep you powered 24/7 even without grid or sun.
